Everything you need to know before getting started

ProTutor receives hundreds of tuition requests every day. This subscription ensures you are prioritised as a serious parent and gives you access to our full-service experience — a verified tutor, a dedicated Relationship Manager, and end-to-end support from day one. Think of it as your commitment to your child\'s learning, backed by ours.

We stand behind our service. If we cannot connect you with a suitable tutor within the committed timeframe, you can raise a refund request after that period. Refunds are processed under our terms and conditions. Your trust matters more to us than the fee.
No, it is a one-time payment for your chosen plan duration. When your plan ends, you decide what happens next. Continue with the same tutor, renew the plan, or take a break. No automatic charges, no pressure.
The moment we receive payment confirmation, our team starts working. We call you first to understand your requirement in detail, then begin shortlisting. Depending on your plan, a tutor is assigned within 3 to 7 working days. You are never left waiting without an update.
We arrange a free demo class before anything is finalised. If it does not feel right, just let your RM know. We shortlist another tutor and arrange a fresh demo at no extra cost. Your child\'s comfort comes before anything else.
You pay only for classes that happen. If the tutor is absent, that class is not billed. For Elite plan members, an online backup tutor is arranged on the same day so learning never stops.
Think of your RM as your personal coordinator at ProTutor. Their number is shared with you on the day of onboarding or the very next day. Got a scheduling conflict? Need a replacement? Have a concern? One call or message to your RM handles it. You never explain your situation to a stranger twice.
Your tutor marks attendance through our app right after every class. You get a notification instantly. Your monthly fee is calculated based on this record alone. No manual tracking, no disputes, full clarity every month.
